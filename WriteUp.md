Our game was heavily influenced by Super Mario Bros. This is why we chose the platformer design with different types of enemies. We adapted this inspiration into a game filled with different types of enemies including slimes which just move around like the Goomba's in Mario and our mages that shoot fireballs at the player which is inspired by the Piranha Plant. However we also added some of our own creations, bats that fly and chase the player and traps that explode when the player steps on them. Additionally, we drew inspiration from Mario's stomp for our combat system. Rather than simply jumping our player swings downwards to defeat enemies below, while also having a horizontal swing for ground level combat.
